Odin Recipes Citrus Pina Colada

A refreshing drink to drink in the summer!

This a non-alcohlic drink that is refreshing and enjoyable to drink by a pool.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can cream of coconut
  • 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced
  • 2 limes, zested and juiced
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine cream of coconut, pineapple, orange zest, lime juice, lime zest, ice and vanilla extract in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a cup and enjoy your citrus pina colada drink!
